Easily Convert Plain text WordPress Post URLs to Hyperlinks

== Description ==

Convert Plain text WordPress Post URLs and email addresses to Hyperlinks.

Are you tired of manually converting plain text URLs in your WordPress posts to clickable hyperlinks? Let Clickable Links do the heavy lifting for you! This plugin seamlessly converts all plain text URLs in your posts to HTML hyperlinks, enhancing the user experience on your website.

The plugin specifically targets plain text URLs in your posts, leaving URLs in WordPress pages untouched. This ensures that your pages remain unaffected by Clickable Links' functionality. Whether your URLs are short-links or full URLs, this plugin is capable of converting them into clickable hyperlinks.

Say goodbye to the tedious task of manually converting URLs in your posts and hello to improved user experience with Clickable Links. Download the plugin today and enjoy the benefits of seamless hyperlink conversion on your website.
